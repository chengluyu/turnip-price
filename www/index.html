AC:HN Price Calculator

Acknowledgement

This work cannot be done without @_Ninji, who disassembled the game executable and published a C++ version of the price calculation algorithm.

How to Use

Generally, you cannot use this to predict turnip prices because you don't have the seed. However, since seed is a unsigned 32-bit integer, we can enumerate all possibility. On my computer, it takes 16 minutes to enumerate all combination of patterns and seeds.

Beside, even if you have the seed. This calculator won't work the first time you buy the turnips, according to @_Ninji's reply.

One thing that can skew it for reports: the FirstKabuPattern flag (set directly after the first day you buy turnips on your own island - i.e. not travelling) forces pattern 3 and generates a new buy price.
